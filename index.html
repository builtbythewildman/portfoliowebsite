About Me

Software Development has always intrigued me because of the problem solving aspect involved but more importantly, the ability to work from any location in the world that has a stable internet connection.

You're probably wondering about the brand name "Built By The Wildman"...

Growing up in South Africa, I spent most of my free time chasing baboons, catching snakes & exploring the vast mountain ranges around my town. I thought this was a normal activity for teenagers until I moved to Europe in 2017. I decided to embrace The Wildman persona and develop a philosophy (and later a lifestyle) around it. One based on the pursuit of freedom, growth and exploration.

In 2019, decided to break into the Tech Industry by learning how to code. Although, at the time, I was a bit unsure if I made the right decision but that all changed when I wrote my first JavaScript function...

Fast forward to the present day, my infatuation with JavaScript & other programming languages has developed into a love story of the ages...
